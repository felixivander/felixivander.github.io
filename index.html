Welcome to my website. I'm Felix.
I am a Quantum Science and Engineering PhD student at Harvard University. Previously, I completed my Master's degree in Theoretical Chemistry at UC Berkeley. Before that, I studied physics and chemistry at the University of Toronto for my undergraduate and wrote my thesis under Prof. Dvira Segal.
I am Indonesian. In my spare time I enjoy working out, playing football or badminton, and watching movies.
Previously, I worked under Prof. David C. Stone in designing an open-access Gas Chromatography Simulator. I spent 1 year working with Katelyn Dixon, supervised by Prof. Nazir P. Kherani and Prof. Naomi Matsuura, in using SERS-ML to diagnose cardiovascular disease biomarkers in blood. I also worked briefly with Remy Ko to functionalize gold substrates for SERS and to examine different Raman elements.
